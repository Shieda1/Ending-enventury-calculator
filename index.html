What is the ending inventory calculator?
The ending inventory calculator is a tool that computes the total value of units in an inventory at the end of an accounting period.
How to calculate ending inventory?
To calculate ending inventory you should use the following formula:
ending inventory = beginning inventory + purchases - cost of sales
Why use ending inventory calculator?
The ending inventory calculator is used in order to determine the total value of units in an inventory at the end of an accounting period.
